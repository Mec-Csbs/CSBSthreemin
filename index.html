  • Presentations are limited to three minutes maximum. Any competitor that exceeds the time limit will be disqualified.
  • Presenters may use no more than three slides. Slides cannot include animations or transitions.
  • No additional props or electronic media are permitted. All slides and presentation materials must be submitted according to deadlines set by the 3MP organizational committee.
  • Presenters may use notes or cue cards.
  • Presentations are considered to have commenced when a presenter starts his or her presentation through either movement or speech.
  • Presentations are to commence from the stage.
  • The decisions of the 3MP competition judges are final.
